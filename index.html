1. Data Collection & Use

Taven is built on a "Privacy First" philosophy. We do not collect, store, or sell your personal information. All task data, notes, and schedules are stored locally on your device using SwiftData. ✓ No accounts required

2. Cloud Syncing

If you choose to enable iCloud syncing, your data is transferred securely via Apple's CloudKit infrastructure. This data is end-to-end encrypted and accessible only through your Apple ID — we have no visibility into it whatsoever.

3. AI Processing

Task prioritization and all AI suggestions are computed entirely on-device. ✓ On-device only Your task content is never sent to external servers for processing or model training. Your thoughts stay yours.

4. Data Deletion

Because your data is stored locally and within your private iCloud account, you have complete control. Deleting the app or clearing your data through the app's settings permanently and irreversibly removes all your information — no deletion requests needed.
